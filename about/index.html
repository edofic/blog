About me

My name is Andraz and I work as a software engineer. Sometimes writing code (mostly Go) but mostly telling others what and how to write. But more importantly I’m a curious human. And when I feel like it I write about my explorations here on this blog.

In my free time I’m often playing around with various statically-typed functional programming “things”. But work has also led me in the direction of distributed systems and low latency which both turned out of great interest as well.
